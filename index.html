Geekssociated Press


I work as an on air personality covering various different geeky events. I love the nerd culture, and work in trying to keep it alive by expanding it's reach over the interwebs. You can find me at any of the various conventions, or even some midnight launches. Check out our coverage HERE!


Heatsync Labs


I volunteer at a hackerspace in Mesa, Arizona called Heatsync Labs. I helped create the website and am currently championing the front windows in an attempt to create an interactive space using projects members have completed. I also teach a few classes at the lab, and give tours reguarly.

hackPHX


HackPHX is a group starting out of heatsync whose mission is to bring hackathons to the Phoenix area. So far, they've put on a HTML 5 game hackathon which I competed in, and more recently they put on an arduino hackathon. I covered the event with Geekssociated Press as well as helped put it on.
